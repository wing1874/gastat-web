Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-12-29

Thank you for using GAStat ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Authorize via Google Sign-In

  • Google Account basic information (such as email address and user ID)
  • Access to your Google Analytics properties and data, as explicitly authorized by you

b. Usage Data

  • Device information (e.g., model, operating system version)
  • In-app behavior (such as screen views and feature usage)
  • Crash reports and performance logs

c. Subscription Information

  • Subscription status and selected plan (monthly or yearly), processed securely through Apple In-App Purchase

2. How We Use Your Information

We use Google user data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users via Google Sign-In and identify their Google account.
  • To access, display, and analyze Google Analytics data selected by the user, solely to provide the core functionality of the App.
  • To enable Pro features related to analytics data visualization and insights.
  • To improve app features, performance, and user experience based on aggregated and anonymized usage insights.

Google user data is not used for advertising purposes and is not combined with data from other services for marketing, profiling, or tracking.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not share, transfer, or disclose Google user data to any third parties.
  • All Google Analytics data accessed by the App is processed locally on the user's device and is not stored on external servers controlled by the developer.
  • The App does not sell, rent, or use Google user data for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.
  • Google user data may only be disclosed if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legally enforceable governmental requests.

4. Data Storage and Protection

  • The App does not permanently store Google Analytics data or Google account credentials on external servers.
  • Authorization tokens are stored locally on the device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms.
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In and Google Analytics APIs
  • Apple In-App Purchase

These services are used only as required to provide the App's core functionality.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account settings.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription through the Apple App Store.
  • Remove locally stored app data by uninstalling the App.
  • Contact us to request data deletion or clarification.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
